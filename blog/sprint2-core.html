My Learning Plan

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

My long term goal is to start my own company and work on projects that I love and that are ethical and work to make a difference. I do however intend to work in a more traditional software developer role first to gain practice with the technologies we use and to get experience working in that environment. I do personally hate bureaucracy and intend to develop and experiment with better ways of working when I work on my own companies.

A description of your own strengths and limitations in relation to learning

I am very curious and passionate about things of interest. A lot of the skills I have are because I was curious enough to experiment and teach myself and that is especially good because I love web dev. I am limited by my ability to be interested in too many different things and find that i get distracted very easily. I can also be quite intense which can make it difficult to work with as i struggle with how to communicate my different opinion.

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at EDA?

I'd like to work on my ability to communicate my point of view and work in a team environment. I'd also like to work on completing tasks when I have set deadlines and agreed to them with a team.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme. This should include:

I will manage my workload by setting the abstract goal of tackling something everyday and working as much as I can. I will scope out what the weeks workload looks like and allocate my work time accordingly.

An explanation of how you will manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, facilitators and industry/community representatives

I will manage myself by setting rough boundaries of time to work on things and stick to those as much as possible. If I go over the alloted time I will let anyone effected know ahead of time if possible.

What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you

I will ask for help if I am stuck and identify which tasks are easiest to start with so I can get into flow and then tackle the bigger tasks.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

I will seek help if I have explored other options and still do not understand something or am about to leave the problem for a later time.

A description of what you expect from the facilitation team

I expect support during normal business hours and help if needed.
