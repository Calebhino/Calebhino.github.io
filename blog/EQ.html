What is emotional Intelligence? Emotional Intelligence is the ability to understand and manage your emotions and responses so that you can relieve stress or respond to situations in more positive and meangingfull ways. Emotional Intelligence is also about being aware of other peoples emotions and use empathy to address the needs of other people. How is it different to IQ? IQ is supposedly a measure of someones relative intelligence, focusing on: A persons Visual and spatial processing, knowledge of the world, fluid reasoning, working memory and short-term memory, and quantitative reasoning. EQ (Emotional Intelligence) is measurement of someones ability to intrepret their emotion as well as others to facilitate higher levels of collaboration. Why is it important? Emotional Intelligence is important because humans are social beings and having a greater emotional intelligence allows an individual to succeed further in professional fields and grow more on a personal level.
